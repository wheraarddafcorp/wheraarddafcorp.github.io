　Oil Cooler Guard

R＆G Oil Cooler Guards are available in a Black，Titanium，Light Blue，Red or Dark Blue powder coat finishes．They are made from pressed aluminium and feature a neat，seamless frame．They improve the looks of your bike and help to protect your expensive and vulnerable oil cooler from flying debris．
No permanent modifications need to be made to the bike．This oil cooler guard is suitable for the BMW S1000RR，S1000XR，S1000R and HP4 (All years)．
R＆G Oil Cooler guards are now available in a range of new exciting colour options (please note，not all colour options are available for all R＆G Oil Cooler guards applications)．

Seperate Radiator Guard (RAD0087) also available for this bike．Please allow an addition time for delivery on Special order Blue oil cooler guard．
