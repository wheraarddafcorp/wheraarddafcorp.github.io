Overview:

DONGJIAN 4K x 2K HDMI audio extractor extracts the digital audio signal from the HDMI input and converts it to 2 channel analog stereo output. Simple solution for your amplifier / soundbar / analog speaker that have no HDMI inputs, and compatible with various HDMI source devices that do not have dedicated audio output.

How to use HDMI Audio Extractor Converter function:

Connect your TV HDMI port to this Audio Converter HDMI output port with HDMI Cable, Connect your source HDMI Port to this Audio Converter HDMI input port with HDMI Cable. Then using L/R cable connect to the soundbar, Speaker, home theater, A/V receivers etc.

If there is no sound or only noise, please set the audio format of the signal source to PCM format.
If the machine is not working properly, we highly recommend using USB power adapter Or plug into the USB port of the signal source
