説明: Product Description

Boost and Steady Internet

This CAT8 Ethernet cable supports bandwidth up to 2000MHz and 40Gbps data transmitting speed, so you can connect to your LAN/WAN segments and networking gear at maximum speeds and surf the net, stream videos, play gaming and other at high speed without worrying about cables causing a mess.

Shielded from Interference

Each of our Ethernet cable is manufactured with 26AWG 4-pair stranded pure copper, RJ45 connector plugs with 50-micron gold-plated contacts with molded strain relief snagless boot, and is S/FTP (Shielded and Foiled Twisted Pair).

High Quality Material

This CAT8 cable is made of 100% premium Oxygen-free copper wire. High quality material maximum protect the copper cable core from break or burst and have a long life. All of our cables are passed by FLUKE Test, It is perfect for outdoor & indoor use.
