Printing Landmarks: Popular Geography and Meisho Zue in Late Tokugawa Japan

著者名: Robert Goree 出版者: Harvard University, Asia Center 出版年: 15 Sep 2020 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackSpanning the fields of book history, travel literature, map history, and visual culture, Printing Landmarks provides a new perspective on Tokugawa-period culture. Robert Goree draws on diverse archival and scholarly sources to explore why meisho zue enjoyed widespread and enduring popularity.
