A horn is a trumpet-shaped device or a horn of different lengths that fits into the air inlet in the intake system of a carburetor or throttle engine. It is designed for
1. Let the air flow into the inlet smoothly and evenly with a flow stream attached to the pipe wall called laminar flow.
