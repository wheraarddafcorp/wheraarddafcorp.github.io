UNIQUE 4ーTRACK BOARD FOR SCOREKEEPING: The 4ーtrack continuous board features precision drilled peg holes and laser engraved scoring. Includes 4 peg starting area, 120 count fullーsized cribbage game board, skunk lines, and a pegーout finish hole.

FUN FOR EVERYONE: Cribbage is known for the cribbage board, or crib, which is used for scorekeeping. The crib contains a series of holes on which the score is tallied by pegs. Points are registered by pegging the score along the cribbage board.
